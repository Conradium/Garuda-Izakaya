About Us

In Garuda Ethnic Izakaya, we blend the vibrant flavors of Asia with the warm, inviting atmosphere of a traditional Japanese izakaya. Located in the bustling district of Shinagawa, Tokyo, offers you a unique culinary experience.

Our diverse menu features an innovative fusion of Asian dishes, from Thai green curry infused with Japanese umami to Indonesian nasi goreng and Mutton spare ribs. Our cozy yet sophisticated setting, adorned with elegant wooden furnishings and traditional decorations, creates the perfect ambiance for casual gatherings and intimate dinners alike.

Complement your meal with our signature cocktails, a wide selection of sake, Japanese whiskey, and Asian-inspired craft beers from our fully stocked bar. At Garuda Ethnic Izakaya, we are dedicated to providing exceptional food, innovative drinks, and outstanding service, making every visit a memorable journey across Asia. Join us for an unforgettable dining experience in the heart of Tokyo.
